Phishing Signs to Watch For:

  • No HTTPS (secure connection).
  • Suspicious domains (e.g., misspellings like "g00gle.com").
  • Keywords like "login", "bank", "verify".
  • Long or complex URLs with redirects.
  • Random numbers/letters (obfuscation).
  • URL shorteners (e.g., bit.ly, is.gd) that hide the real link.

Always verify URLs before clicking!
